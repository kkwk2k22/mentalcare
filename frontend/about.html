Our Mission

MentalCare was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide students with accessible, evidence-based tools and resources to support their mental well-being and academic success.

We believe that mental health is just as important as academic achievement, and that supporting one enhances the other.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MentalCare really free?

Yes! MentalCare is completely free for all students. We believe mental health resources should be accessible to everyone.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We use encryption and follow strict privacy protocols. Your personal information is never shared.

Can I use MentalCare on my phone?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. MentalCare provides support tools but is not a substitute for professional mental health care when needed.
